Programme Overview

The BCA (New) programme at IGNOU aims to provide students who have completed 10+2 with a comprehensive foundation in computing and IT. Graduates can pursue MCA or other master’s programmes, or enter entry-level careers in IT/ITES sectors.

Job Roles and Career Paths- After completing your BCA, you can apply for a variety of entry-level positions. The most common and popular roles include: Software Developer/Engineer: This is one of the most common paths. You would be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining software applications using various programming languages. Web Developer: With skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, you can become a web developer, creating and maintaining websites and web applications. Data Analyst: This role involves collecting, cleaning, and interpreting data to help businesses make informed decisions. It's a growing field, and a BCA provides a solid foundation. Technical Analyst: You would use your IT skills to analyze technical issues and recommend solutions to improve existing systems. Cybersecurity Specialist: With the increasing number of cyber threats, this field is in high demand. You would work to protect an organization's network and data. Other Roles: Other potential jobs include Network Administrator, Database Administrator, IT Support Executive, and Cloud Support Associate.

Eligibility, Duration & Fees

  • Eligibility: 10+2 or Diploma in Commercial Practice or equivalent.
  • Duration: Minimum 3 years, maximum 6 years.
  • Fees: ₹12,000/- per semester plus applicable registration and development fees.
